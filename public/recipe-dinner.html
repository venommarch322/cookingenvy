Creamy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta until tender.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cream.
  4. Mix with pasta and serve warm.
