Berry Oat Breakfast Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Add oats to two bowls.
  2. Top with yogurt and berries.
  3. Drizzle honey and sprinkle chia seeds.
  4. Serve immediately.
