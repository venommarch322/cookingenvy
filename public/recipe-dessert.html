Simple Sweet Dessert

An easy homemade dessert that looks elegant and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking dish and bake for 20 minutes or until golden.
  5. Let cool slightly and serve.

Serving Tip

Serve with fresh berries, whipped cream, or a light dusting of powdered sugar for a simple finishing touch.
